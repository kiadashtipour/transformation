Conspiracy Theories Against Religious Groups

The entry looks at the conspiracy theories constructed against various religious groups, especially at the critical context of the pandemic. The hatred campaigns, fake news and misinformation against the religious groups come under this entry.

Conspiracy in India (Virus Jihad)

Case Description

The Tablighi Jamaat conference started on 3rd March 2020 few days before the lockdown started in India on 29th March 2020. The conference in Delhi was blamed by BJP (Bharatiya Janata Party) officials as a mission to spread Covid_19 virus in India, the Corona Jihad/Virus Jihad.

Functions

Exclusive; Deliberate; Incremental (downwards); Regressive

The Corona Jihad/Virus Jihad conspiracy was used by the BJP officials exclusively to target the Muslim minorities in India. Such conspiracy produces misunderstanding and communal gap. Regressive from SED 3 (Good Health and Well-Being); 10 (Reduced Inequalities).
