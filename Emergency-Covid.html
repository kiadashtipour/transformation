The entry focuses on emergency health care initiative like hospitals and/or units to fight the spread of Covid_19 virus. Interesting stories of rapid Corona mitigation plans come under this section.

Emergency Covid_19 Hospital in Kerala

Case Description

Tata Group India built 15 crores (Indian rupee) emergency Hospital with the capacity of 540 beds, isolation wards, ICU and other facilities in the border district of Kerala, Kasargod under the Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiative of Tata Group.

SDG Targets

3 (Good Health and Well-Being); 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth) 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure); 10 (Reduced Inequalities); 17 (Partnership for the public benefit)

Functions

Inclusive; deliberate; incremental; top-down; progressive

The initiative inclusively renders emergency health care and security for the people of the border district Kasargod, as the district was deprived of the better health institutions. The emergency Covid_19 hospital was built in the partnership of Kerala government and Tata group India. The hospital offers job opportunities and economic growth for the border district, as the hospital is one of the signs of the development of the district.
