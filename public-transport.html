Public Transport

Public transport is disrupted during the pandemic for various reasons, either due to lockdown measures or due to the need to take precautionary measures to avoid the further spread of the virus.

Case description


On May 20, 2020, Kerala State Water Transport Department arranged a 70-seater boat for a lone student for writing school exams. A student named Sandra (Class 11) from Alappuzha district went to her school to attend the exams using the boat service deployed by the government. The boat was arranged to a non-service route just for her so that she won’t miss the examinations. Sandra lives in a small Island in Alappuzha district and was depending on private motorized canoes. Due to lockdown there was no means of transportation from her village. However due the support from Kerala government she completed all her exams. 


Functions

deliberate; sudden; top bottom, Progressive
This act of government is inclusive as it helped a student from geographically marginalized place to attend her exams amid pandemic. They deliberately addressed the issue faced by a school student and took necessary actions. It is a sudden action taken by the Kerala State Water Transport Department. Hence a sudden response to the issues faced by a student during pandemic directed top-down, from state department. This is progressive in targeting three SDGs, safeguarding the educational quality, women empowerment and equality.
