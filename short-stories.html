Short Stories

This entry focuses on the short stories published around the world during the Covid_19 pandemic. The sections in the entry are specified for the inspiring short stories got published to provide mental and/or physical support for the community to cope up with the pandemic.

Case description


The Inter-Agency Standing Committee Reference Group on Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (IASC MHPSS RG) developed a story book titled “My Hero is You” for children to help the families to deal with the difficult situation during the Covid_19 pandemic. “My Hero is You” conveys the psychological method for children and parents to deal with the pandemic. The story book helps to assess children’s mental and psychological health during the pandemic outbreak. The kids book offers parenting techniques to deliver quality education to develop strong social institutions.

SDG Targets

3 (Good Health and Well-Being); 4 (Quality Education)


Functions

deliberate; sudden; top bottom, Progressive
The short story promotes parenting methods to build an inclusive atmosphere in the house and family, then the society. The book was deliberately published during the critical time of the pandemic to improve the mental and physical health of the kids. Taking the concern of health into consideration, this attempt is an incremental effort to enhance the strength of the children. IASC published My Hero is You meets the SDG goals ‘Good Health and Well-Being; Quality Education’ providing effective information regarding the mental and physical health of kids. Thus, the effort is a top-down approach.
